About Me (CV)

I am a final-year PhD student in the BioMedIA Group at Imperial College London, supervised by Prof. Daniel Rueckert and Dr. Paul Bentley. My research focuses on medical image analysis, computer vision, and machine learning. I have been a research assistant jointly in the Department of Computing and the Division of Brain Sciences, Department of Medicine at Imperial College London since 2014.

In 2013, I obtained an MSc degree with Distinction from the Department of Computing, Imeprial College London. In 2012, I received a BSc degree from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Nanjing Universify of Information Science and Technology.
